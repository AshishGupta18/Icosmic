HACKEYE

An idiosyncratic hackathon (online + hackathon) that focuses on the development of soft skills along with core coding skills.The evaluation criteria is divided into 3 stages. All the participants are supposed to choose a problem statement revolving around current affairs in different disciplines in order to develop a technically achievable solution (in the form of web apps , websites , android apps , etc.). The participants have to come prepared with a proposal speech of their product . The pitch must contain the problem statement , solution approach , revenue model (if any), UI screenshots , Features & USP in a ppt.The pitch shall be presented in front of a bench of highly experienced industrial experts/influencers for evaluation. The participants will then provide a documented prototype of their idea through Project workflow , use case diagrams , activity diagram and user stories. The remaining time of the hackathon shall be dedicated to the development of the proposed project until the final evaluation. All the participants shall be provided with refreshments periodically , they will also have access to all the food stalls in BU. All the participants shall be provided with yoga mats , neck pillows , bean bags,extension cords and unlimited coffee , for their convenience.

ROUND 1 :

All the participants will be given a code problem chosen from a question pool. They will be free to code in the language of their choice. This round will test their logical approach to solving a problem.


ROUND 2 :

This round's goal is to assess students' versatility and compatibility with using different programming languages in visual paradigms. Participants will be paired up and given a problem with the target language mentioned. As soon as the first team member finds the solution, their partner receives their code but not the test case or problem statement. The teammate will now need to convert the coding language into a different language (will be mentioned to the participant separately). The output of the original code and the modified code must be the same for all the test cases. Participants are free to choose the task they want to perform. Scores are to be displayed on the leaderboard.


ROUND 3 :

A rapid-fire round to check the speed and accuracy of the participants. It is 1v1. They will be given a buzzer, and the first person to hit it will be the winner if their code is correct. Winner's score shall be raised immediately on the leaderboard.


ROUND 4 :

User requirements will be given in the form of riddles and user stories. The participants will come up with the most suitable design (non coded: design & flow only). They are free to upgrade their UI as they like. Evaluation will be done by Web Developers. Bonus points shall be awarded. Any boost in scores will be updated in the leaderboard ranking.

The one who scores highest points without being eliminated shall be the winner!
