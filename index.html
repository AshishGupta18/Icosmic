About US

Unless the yang turns bold autumn for yin to remember the dark, the chess play doesn't get rid of the boundaries & the queen doesn't rule the rules.- Sana Sharma (President of ATC)

Icosmic-a phantasm of the mind becoming a reality, is a two-day inter-college tech gala at Bennett University (The Times of India Group), Greater Noida, that will be organised on February 4 and 5, 2023.

From hands-on workshops to hackathons to real-life problem-solving competitions, it features a range of tech activities that are meant to amuse while also testing their skills and fostering the development of every engineering student, coordinated by well-reputed student clubs of SCSET BU: the Alan Turing Club, Bennett Cloud Computing Club, Fullstack Club, IEEE, Ciphers, IBF, Codechef, and PDT. So join the Icosmic family and seize the chance to receive LORs from renowned and respected personalities as well as cash prizes and other goodies. We're excited to see what new ideas and breakthroughs will come out of this year's event.

Aim

When it comes to innovation, an ounce of execution is worth more than a ton of theory.

Science and engineering are driven by purpose. Like artists, engineers leave an aesthetic trail of ideas bifurcating and evolving through rough sketches, complex drawings, models, and mockups. Our aim is to conduct several workshops in order to brush up on the skills and then assess their level of comprehension, problem solving aptitude, and other qualities of an engineering student to make them know which side their bread is buttered and get their foot in the door.

Rules and Regulations

1

Registrations shall be open till 2nd of February, 2023 and after that no participant would be able to do so.

2

No participant is allowed to help any other participant, and if found doing so, it will be considered as unfair means.

3

Participants must bring their valid College ID cards and the receipt of registration during reporting. Otherwise, the participant may be disqualified.

4

The use of internet or other resources for solution shall be considered as unfair means. The plagiarised content shall be dissqualifed immediately.

5

Penalty System: 2 Yellow stamps as a warning and red stamp afterwards as eliminator and will be out if the participant is found with prohibited or unethical substances

6

For any undisciplinary action and usage of unfair means, the participant will be imposed with yellow stamp as warning and after two yellow stamp they will be eliminated with a red stamp.
